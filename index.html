What happens next

  1. Your report is stored in the system for staff review.
  2. Protective Services compares your report with found items submitted to the office.
  3. If staff need more details, they can contact you using your email or phone number.
  4. If a likely match is found, you may be asked to verify identifying details before pickup.
  5. Items turned in to Lost & Found are generally held for about two weeks.
