Lost something on campus? Start here.

CampusFind gives students, visitors, and campus users a simple way to log in, report lost items, and securely follow their claim status.

Log in to report
  • Anyone who loses an item can create an account and request help.
  • Found items should be handed directly to Protective Services.
  • Only admin staff manage found-item records and matching.
